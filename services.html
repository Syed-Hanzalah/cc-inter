      International Trade Services

      In the port and maritime equipment sector , we work closely with several trusted and renowned business partners. These strategic partnerships allow us to provide our customers with state-of-the-art equipment and technologies such as:

      Heavy lifting equipment

      Rail cranes and port tyred cranes, container and ore gantry cranes, grain gantry cranes

      Equipment for the control

      Equipment for the control and fight against marine pollution: floating booms, dispersants, skimmers, storage, first response kits, etc.

      Maritime buoyage devices

      Buoys, lights, AIS, remote control system, etc. Navigation safety devices : VTS/VTMIS

      In the field of international trade, C C INTER is committed to offering high quality products adapted to the particular needs of its customers, ensuring optimal performance and exceptional reliability. Our network of partners allows us to guarantee innovative and efficient solutions for the maritime sector.

      Consulting Services

      At C C INTER, we use the diverse expertise of our specialists to conduct general studies in the maritime field. Our multidisciplinary skills allow us to carry out the following missions on behalf of our clients:

      Port terminal feasibility studies

      We carry out in-depth technical and financial analyses to ensure the viability and efficiency of port infrastructure.

      Strategic studies on port auxiliary operations

      We conduct studies on essential topics such as waste management, management of towing activities, pollution prevention, and many other critical areas for the proper functioning of ports.

      Port Security Consulting Engagements:

      In compliance with the ISPS Code and International Maritime Organization (IMO) regulations, we offer consulting services to enhance port security and ensure the protection of maritime infrastructure and operations. In particular for Security and Safety Audit engineering.

      C C INTER is able to carry out:

      Signalling Studies :Analysis and optimisation of signalling systems for precise navigation.

      Maritime Safety :Design of safety protocols and systems to prevent accidents at sea.

      Port security :Robust security measures, surveillance and access control for port infrastructure.

      Security Audit :Complete audits of port organizations and activities to identify vulnerabilities and propose solutions.

      In the field of consulting, C C INTER is committed to providing innovative solutions adapted to the specific needs of each client, based on internationally recognized quality and safety standards.
