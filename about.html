ABOUT
Founded in 2012 and incorporated in 2023, CCINTER is a recognized consulting and trading services provider, specializing in projects related to the port and maritime sectors. Our extensive individual and collective experience in project management and partnerships with leading providers around the world, has allowed us to develop in-depth expertise in various port and maritime contexts and environments .
At CCINTER, we recognize the critical importance of sustainability in all industries. We are committed to being at the forefront of innovative trends that improve the efficiency and performance of port management. Our commitment to sustainability guides our constant efforts to implement best practices, for the benefit of our customers and the environment.
We are proud of our strong network of reliable business partners, consisting of leaders in the field of equipment manufacturing as well as experts in port and marine safety and security. This collaboration allows us to provide our customers with state-of-the-art solutions and quality service.
CCINTER has built a robust customer base beyond Canada's borders and is now focused on expanding its operations in Canada. We aim to serve both public and private clients, offering expert consulting services that meet their unique needs and challenges.
Join us as we navigate the future of maritime business with innovation, expertise and an unwavering commitment to sustainability.
Our Goals
At CCINTER, we strive to explore and adopt new approaches in order to continuously improve port management skills. We are committed to staying at the forefront of innovation in this space, integrating advanced and sustainable solutions that meet the evolving needs of our customers.
Our goal is to become a recognized leader in the field of marine projects , providing consulting services and business solutions that not only meet current requirements but also anticipate future challenges. We are committed to making a significant contribution to the sustainability and efficiency of port operations, both in Canada and internationally.
