ABOUT

Established in 2012 and incorporated in 2023, CCINTER is a trusted provider of consulting and negotiation services specializing in the port and maritime sectors. With extensive collective expertise in project management and strategic partnerships with global industry leaders, we excel in diverse port and maritime environments.

At CCINTER, sustainability is at the core of our mission. We are committed to driving innovation that enhances efficiency and performance in port management while adopting best practices to benefit both our clients and the environment.

Our trusted network includes leading equipment manufacturers and maritime safety experts, enabling us to deliver tailored, high-quality solutions. With a strong international presence, we are now focused on expanding our operations in Canada to meet the unique needs of both public and private clients.

Join CCINTER as we navigate the future of the maritime sector with innovation, expertise, and a steadfast commitment to sustainability.

Our Vision
At CCINTER, we continuously innovate to enhance port management expertise, integrating advanced and sustainable solutions to meet evolving client needs. Committed to excellence, we aim to be a leader in maritime consulting, delivering forward-thinking solutions that drive sustainability and efficiency in port operations, both in Canada and globally.

International Trade Services

In the port and maritime equipment sector , we work closely with several trusted and renowned business partners. These strategic partnerships allow us to provide our customers with state-of-the-art equipment and technologies such as:

Port handling equipment

STS container, Harbour cranes, Rail and rubber-tyred cranes, Grain ship unloaders.

Equipment for the protection of the marine environment.

Equipment for the control and fight against marine pollution: floating booms, dispersants, skimmers, storage, first response kits, etc.

Maritime buoyage devices

Buoys, lights, AIS, Aids to Navigation AtoN , VTS/VTMIS.

In the field of maritime international trade, CCINTER is committed to offering products adapted to the particular needs of its customers, ensuring optimal performance and exceptional reliability. Our network of partners allows us to guarantee innovative and efficient solutions for the maritime sector.

Consulting Services

At CCINTER, we use the diverse expertise of our specialists to conduct general studies in the field of port and maritime projects. Our multidisciplinary skills allow us to carry out the following missions on behalf of our clients:

Port terminal feasibility studies

We carry out in-depth technical and financial analyses to ensure the viability and efficiency of port infrastructure.

Technical and Strategic studies on port auxiliary operations

We conduct studies on essential topics such as waste management, management of towing activities, pollution prevention, and many other critical areas for the proper functioning of ports.

Port Safety and Security Consulting:

In compliance with the ISPS Code and International Maritime Organization (IMO) regulations, we offer consulting services to enhance port safety and security to ensure the protection of maritime infrastructure and operations. In particular for Security and Safety Audit engineering.

In the field of consulting, CCINTER is committed to providing innovative solutions adapted to the specific needs of each client, based on internationally recognized quality and safety standards.
